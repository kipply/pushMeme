How It Works

flash_on

Keep Organized and Track Your Own Goals

Individual and group goals are set for a due date and can be broken down into smaller tasks to do each with their own due date. A progress bar at the top shows how far tasks and goals are coming along. PushMe.me keeps you on track for achieving both your group goals and your personal goals.

group

Achieve Goals With Friends

By tracking individual progress and overall group progress, group members can keep each other accountable. If someone is not performing at their best, a meme of encouragement can be sent. If someone is performing well beyond expectations, a meme of celebration can be delivered.

settings

Spread Memes of Encouragement

Encouragement on PushMe.me is supplied through the generation of memes and inspirational messages. Happy, funny memes of all sorts are sent when an individual/group is staying on task. Anonymous messages can also be sent individually to cheer on people with their productivity.
